Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an offline personal interview on campus. This is an opportunity to demonstrate your passion, technical knowledge, and fit within our community.

Official Induction
Successful candidates will be officially inducted as CodeChef Club members and embark on an incredible journey of growth, learning, and achievement! 🎉
